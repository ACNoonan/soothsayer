The humility slide · push back on any of these

Risks and honest unknowns.

01
Currently evaluating market gaps. If Chainlink’s stale hold is already tight enough versus Monday’s open, our core anchor loses weight.
02
Single-publisher credibility gap. Pyth ships 100+ institutional publishers. We ship one model. Multi-publisher trust has to come through replication, not BD.
03
RedStone is iterating on the same space. $840M FDV, Securitize + BUIDL + ACRED, and Live 24/7 shipped March 2026. They could publish methodology first.
04
Distribution is owned by Chainlink. xStocks Alliance, Kamino, Ondo rollout. Our path is secondary observation, not primary replacement.
05
CTA / UTP licensing is gray. Futures-primary input stack sidesteps SIP by design; cash NBBO stays calibration-only. Still a surface worth naming.
06
Kamino TVL is still small. $25M xStock supply today. The growth story is unlocking capital efficiency, not capturing fees on existing flow.
