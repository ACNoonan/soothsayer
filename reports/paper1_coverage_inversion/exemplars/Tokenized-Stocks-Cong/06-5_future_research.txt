remaining within a walled garden. Absent interoperability, such designs risk fragmenting liquidity across platforms.

this small market. Conversely, if both succeed, their presence may attract others.

6.5

Future Research Directions
The phenomenon of tokenized assets opens many avenues for research. Based on our findings, some potential

directions arise:
1. A deeper analysis of price discovery using intraday data: for example, Hasbrouck’s information share between the token market and underlying market could be computed, similar to studies comparing ADRs
with their home markets.
2. Impact on volatility: future research could examine if stocks that are tokenized have different overnight
volatility or opening gap behavior than those that are not tokenized, controlling for firm characteristics.
Currently, many major ones are tokenized, but one could find some that are not and compare.
