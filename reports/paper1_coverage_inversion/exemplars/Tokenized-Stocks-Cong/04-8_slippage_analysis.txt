Slippage Analysis: Daily Maximum Deviation
“Slippage” here refers to the daily maximum percentage deviation of the off-hour tokenized price from the

underlying stock’s last close. We compute, for each day t, the largest absolute gap between the xStock price and
the last closing price of the underlying stock during the non-trading interval, defined as 16:00 on day t to 09:00 on
