coupling during normal periods. On Saturday and Sunday, the distributions are slightly wider—tokens more frequently trade a few percent away from the last close. Importantly, extreme deviations were rare and almost always
tied to specific news events. The largest positive token premium (≈14%) corresponds to the pre-market news on
